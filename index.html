OVERVIEW | CONTROLS | RULES

This is a classic arcade-style space shooter where you pilot a spaceship to defend against waves of enemies.

Your goal is to survive as long as possible, destroy enemies, and progress through multiple rounds — each with unique formations and attack patterns.

Enemies get faster, stronger, and more aggressive as you advance, culminating in boss battles and a final mothership fight.

KEYACTION
← Move to left
→ Move to right
Spacebar Shoot regular bullets
Z Activate Super Ball Attack
X Heal 10 HP
ESC Return to main menu

1. Goal: Destroy all enemies and survive each round.


2. Rounds: Enemies appear in different formations — later rounds are harder.


3. HP & Lives: Losing all HP costs one life. Game over when all lives are gone.


4. Score: Earn points for every enemy destroyed.


5. Victory: Clear all rounds to win the game.

User arrow key to navigate and esc to exit
